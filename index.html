Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our locksmith services

How much does a locksmith cost in Austin, TX?

Locksmith pricing depends on the service type, time of day, and hardware involved. At Fast Austin Locksmith, we provide honest, transparent pricing before starting any work. No surprises.

Can a locksmith make a key without the original?

Yes. In many cases, we can create a new key even if you lost the original. For vehicles, we use professional key-cutting and programming equipment. For homes and businesses, we can rekey or cut keys based on the lock cylinder.

Do you program key fobs and push-to-start keys?

Yes. We provide smart key programming, push-to-start key programming, and transponder key services for many vehicle makes and models. Modern cars require advanced equipment ‚Äî and we come fully prepared.

Is it better to rekey or replace locks?

Rekeying is often the smarter and more affordable solution if your existing hardware is still in good condition. Rekeying changes the internal pins so old keys no longer work, giving you full control again without replacing the entire lock.
